01 About

Driven by purpose,
guided by strategy.

Jonathan Ran is the Chief Operating Officer and Co-Founder of Neobotics Foundation Inc., a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to making robotics education accessible to underserved communities.

With a foundation in the liberal arts and a sharp operational mind, Jonathan bridges the gap between mission-driven work and scalable execution — overseeing partnerships, social media strategy, and the day-to-day operations that keep Neobotics running.

"Technology education isn't a privilege — it's a right. Every student deserves access to the tools that will shape their future."
